About

I was a Canadian Member of Parliament for the New Democratic Party between 2011 and 2015, and have served in the shadow cabinet as deputy critic for the Environment, deputy critic for Science and Technology and deputy critic for International Trade. I have tabled three bills and one motion, including legislation to protect unpaid interns and a bill to make enrolment in the Guaranteed Income Supplement automatic. I have initiated and carried out campaigns on consumer rights, poverty, employment and health.

I am currently pursuing a MSc in Human Rights at the London School of Economics and Political Science.
